Covid-19 Update & How Dotty is Helping

As of April 6, 3,660 Filipinos have been confirmed to have COVID-19. The rise in numbers has then prompted the government to put cities on lockdown, later leading to citizens buying as much supplies as they can. Apart from food, people have also stocked up on medical supplies - items that healthcare professionals need the most.
