The Year
in Review .

This year is a year like no other. One bad break after the other. Natural catastrophes, political unrest and a global pandemic has changed how we live and work. Having to work from home while supporting our clients and dealing with lockdowns was not easy - but the team, collaborating with our clients, made it work. Delivering great work despite the circumstances.



Growth was also a focus for this year. At the beginning of the year we had a solid plan for expansion, but had to adjust because of the lockdowns. We solidified our management team and made some restructuring. Patrick delos Reyes has been promoted to lead the Manila team as the Associate Operations Manager. Jose Antonio Perez has assumed the position of Lead Back-End Developer. Moving from Creative to Project Management, Gezzy Aurell Maglalang is now our Creative PM. Christine Sicad from HR & Admin promoted to Project & Traffic Coordinator. A well deserved congratulations!



There were a lot of great projects this year. It was great to share honors with one of our long standing clients The Economist in winning People's Voice winner for Best User Interface in the 24th Webby Awards for the Safe Cities 2019 Website ( safecities.economist.com ). Definitely a proud moment for the team and is a testament to everyone’s creativity and hard work. Here are some of the projects we’ve done this year.





Lastly, we say goodbye to our Manila HQ for the past 5 years. As the Philippines struggle to keep up with the CORONA virus, we decided to keep the team safe by letting them work from home. This means letting go of our office in Makati and delaying our search for a new home til mid next year.
